OUR STORY

Stay Fresh

Fresh Up Barbershop started as a belief — that every man deserves a fresh start, and a great cut is how it begins. Founded right here in San Diego, we've built a reputation on clean lines, sharp fades, and real relationships with every client who sits in our chair.

From local legends to first-timers, we treat every head the same — with precision, pride, and premium product. Our barbers have honed their craft through years of dedication and a genuine love for the trade.
